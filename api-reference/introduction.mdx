## Key Features

1. **Fast Integration**: Simple RESTful API with clear documentation to get you up and running quickly.

2. **Flexible Payments**: Accept a wide range of payment methods including cards, digital wallets, and more, providing convenience for your customers.

3. **Secure Transactions**: Industry-leading security measures are built-in to protect your transactions and customer data.

4. **Comprehensive Tools**: Manage payments, refunds, subscriptions, and payouts seamlessly with our robust set of tools.

5. **Scalable Infrastructure**: Our APIs are designed to scale with your business, ensuring reliable performance even as your transaction volume grows.

6. **Detailed Analytics**: Gain insights into your payment operations with detailed reporting and analytics.

7. **Global Reach**: Expand your business internationally with support for multiple currencies and localized payment methods.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Register on the Dodo Payments Dashboard.

2. **Get API Keys**: Access your test and live credentials.

3. Use the correct Host names for Test mode and Live mode below:
   Test Mode: [`https://test.dodopayments.com`](https://test.dodopayments.com)
   Live Mode: [`https://live.dodopayments.com`](https://live.dodopayments.com)

4. **Test & Deploy**: Use our `Test Mode` to finalize your integration before going live.

Explore the `API Reference Docs` for detailed guides and examples to start processing payments effortlessly.
