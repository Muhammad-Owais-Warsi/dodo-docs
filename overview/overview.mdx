 This feature allows merchants to manage their product offerings, whether they’re one-time purchases or subscription-based services. You can create, configure, and manage products with flexible pricing options and billing cycles to suit your business model.
</Card>
<Card title="Payments" img="/images/our-features/image 2.png" >
 The Payments feature is the backbone of Dodo Payments, handling everything from payment processing to security and fraud protection. This section provides full transparency on how payments are processed, invoiced, and protected.
</Card>
<Card title="Invoicing" img="/images/our-features/image 3.png" >
 Dodo Payments offers Automatic Invoice Generation, simplifying the billing process by automating invoices after every successful transaction. Merchants can also customize the invoice templates to align with their business branding
</Card>
<Card title="Disputes & Refunds" img="/images/our-features/image 4.png" >
 This feature helps merchants manage disputes and refund requests in an organized and timely manner. The Disputes & Refunds feature helps ensure customer satisfaction while maintaining business control over financial operations.
</Card>
<Card title="Payouts" img="/images/our-features/image 5.png" >
The Payouts feature simplifies the process of transferring funds from your Dodo Payments account to your bank account. This section ensures that merchants have full control over their earnings and understand any fees involved.
</Card>
<Card title="User & Access Management" img="/images/our-features/image 6.png" >
Dodo Payments offers Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), allowing merchants to invite team members and assign specific permissions based on their role within the company. This feature ensures that sensitive information is protected while team members have access to the tools they need to perform their duties.
</Card>
<Card title="Analytics & Reporting" img="/images/our-features/image 7.png" >
The Analytics & Reporting feature gives merchants detailed insights into their business’s performance. View comprehensive reports on revenue, customer activity, transaction success rates, and more, all from the Dodo Payments dashboard.
</Card>
<Card title="Customers" img="/images/our-features/image 8.png" >
The Customers Page gives merchants a central view of all customer details
</Card>
<Card title="Fraud Detection & Data  Security" img="/images/our-features/image 8.png" >
Our comprehensive platform follows all standard security and fraud detection practices to ensure transactions are secure
</Card>
