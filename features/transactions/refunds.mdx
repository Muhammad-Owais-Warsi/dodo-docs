## Initiate Refund

<Card title="Refunds" horizontal icon="rotate-left" href="/guides/refund-guide">Process to access refund records on the dashboard</Card>

Merchants can initiate a refund by clicking the **Initiate Refund** button in the Payment Details Page. The refund will only proceed if the refund rules are met.

### Details Required for Refund

- **Refund Amount**: The amount to be refunded.
- **Refund Reason**: The reason for the refund, to be recorded in the system.

### Refund Rules

1. The payment must have been successful.
2. Refunds must be initiated within the payout period (currently 30 days).
3. The refund amount must be less than or equal to the original payment amount.
4. The refund amount must be less than or equal to the merchantâ€™s Dodo Payments account balance.
5. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used by the customer.

If any rule is violated, the refund request will be denied.
