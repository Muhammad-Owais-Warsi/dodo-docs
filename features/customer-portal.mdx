## How it works

Your customers can access the Customer Portal through a link that you share with them. This can be either a static or a dynamic link.

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/customer-portal/link.png" alt="Link Creation"/>
</Frame>

## Static Link

A static link allows any customer to request access to the Customer Portal by entering their email address. This link never expires.

<Steps>
<Step title="Merchant Flow">
    1. Go to Sales→Customer.
    2. Click on <i>share invite</i>.
    3. Copy the link under <i>Static link</i> to share it with your customer.
</Step>

<Step title="Customer Flow">
    1. Customer visits the static link you share.
    2. Customer enters their email address.
    3. Receives a link to log in to their Customer Portal.

    <Check>
    Customers who already have an account will be recognized automatically.
    </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Dynamic Link

A dynamic link is a personalized, one-time link that grants direct access to the Customer Portal. This magic link expires in 24 hours.

<Warning>
Dynamic links expire after 24 hours. Generate a new link if it has expired.
</Warning>

<Steps>
<Step title="Merchant Flow">
    1. Go to Sales→Customer.
    2. Click on <i>share invite</i>.
    3. Copy the link under <i>Dynamic link</i> to share it with your customer.
</Step>

<Step title="Customer Flow">
    1. Customer visits the <i>dynamic link</i> you share.
    2. They are taken directly to the Customer Portal without needing an access link.
</Step>
</Steps>
