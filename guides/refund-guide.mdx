    <img src="/images/guides/refund/Refund 1.webp" alt="View the status"/>

## Step 2: Click on Initiate Refund button for the transaction you want to initiate a refund for

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/guides/refund/Refund 2.webp" alt="Initiate Refund"/>

## Step 3: Fill the refund details and click Initiate Refund button

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/guides/refund/Refund 3.webp" alt="Fill refund details"/>
</Frame>

## Step 4: Refund is initiated and you can view the status of the refund on the Transactions Page → Refunds Tab

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/guides/refund/Refund 4.webp" alt="View refund status"/>
</Frame>
